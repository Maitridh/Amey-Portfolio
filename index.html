Overview

Operation Dead Silence drops you into a high-stakes stealth-action battlefield where every decision matters. Playing as an elite operative, your mission is to infiltrate deep enemy territory, locate a hidden hard drive marked by a flag, extract the critical data, and get out alive.

The game emphasizes tactical movement, environmental awareness, and combat adaptability. Armed enemies patrol dynamic routes, forcing players to choose between silent takedowns or direct confrontations — each with meaningful consequences for mission success.

Jump Quest is a dynamic 2.5D platformer prototype built in Unreal Engine. The game follows a nimble hero navigating across increasingly complex environmental obstacles — leaping between floating platforms, evading unique creatures, and mastering new movement abilities as the adventure unfolds.

Designed as a prototype showcase, Jump Quest demonstrates tight platformer physics, layered environment design, and a progression-based mechanic system where players unlock and learn new moves throughout each level.

Overview

Lost in Voids is a narrative-driven experience built entirely within 6 hours for a game jam. The game follows Ethan — a young man trapped inside the surreal, ever-shifting landscapes of his own subconscious mind.

Caught in an endless dream loop, Ethan must explore fragmented memories, solve environmental puzzles, and piece together the truth of his waking reality. The game blends dreamlike visuals with atmospheric storytelling, creating an unsettling yet poignant journey through the mind.

Overview

Shadow Knight vs Khaimera is a cinematic environment and character composition piece created in Unreal Engine 5. The project explores dramatic environmental storytelling — placing two iconic characters from the gaming world in an original, hand-crafted battleground.

The goal was to push cinematic lighting, real-time rendering, and atmospheric depth to showcase production-quality environmental shots. Every element — from material layering to sky atmosphere — was crafted to serve the dramatic narrative of the confrontation.

Overview

Lost Horizon is a personal Unreal Engine 5 passion project, deeply inspired by the visual language of the Star Wars universe. Created as a dedicated practice ground for cinematography, advanced environment design, and the Metahuman character workflow.

The project explores desolate alien landscapes, dramatic horizon lighting, and narrative-driven camera work. Every shot was composed with a filmmaker's eye — using UE5's cinematic tools to craft frames that feel like stills pulled from a feature film.
